# Knowledge Tree Corporate Contributor License Agreement

Thank you for your organization's interest in contributing to Knowledge Tree
(the "Project"), maintained by OpenKTree Contributors ("We" or "Us").

This Corporate Contributor License Agreement ("Agreement") documents the rights
granted by corporate contributors to Us. This agreement allows an entity (the
"Corporation") to authorize Contributions to the Project and to grant copyright
and patent licenses.

## 1. Definitions

**"Corporation"** means the entity that is making this Agreement with Us,
including all affiliates that control, are controlled by, or are under common
control with that entity. For the purposes of this definition, "control" means
(i) the power, direct or indirect, to cause the direction or management of such
entity, whether by contract or otherwise, or (ii) ownership of fifty percent
(50%) or more of the outstanding shares, or (iii) beneficial ownership of such
entity.

**"Contribution"** means any original work of authorship, including any
modifications or additions to an existing work, that is intentionally submitted
by an Authorized Contributor on behalf of the Corporation to the Project for
inclusion in, or documentation of, any of the products owned or managed by Us.
For the purposes of this definition, "submitted" means any form of electronic,
verbal, or written communication sent to the Project or its representatives,
including but not limited to communication on electronic mailing lists, source
code control systems, and issue tracking systems that are managed by, or on
behalf of, the Project for the purpose of discussing and improving the Project,
but excluding communication that is conspicuously marked or otherwise designated
in writing by the Corporation as "Not a Contribution."

**"Authorized Contributor"** means an employee or contractor of the Corporation
who has been authorized by the Corporation to make Contributions on its behalf.

**"Project"** means the Knowledge Tree software and associated documentation
available at https://github.com/openktree/knowledge-tree.

## How to Sign

To sign the Corporate CLA, an authorized representative of your organization
should email openktree@gmail.com with:

1. Corporation name and address
2. Point of contact name and email
3. List of authorized GitHub usernames (or "all employees")
4. A statement that the representative has authority to enter this agreement

Individual employees covered by a Corporate CLA must still comment on their
pull request with:

> I have read the CLA Document and I hereby sign the CLA
